Chef Bio

Hot-tempered chef Gordon Ramsay owns a number of successful restaurants but is best known as the star of television series Hell's Kitchen.Born in Scotland in 1966, chef Gordon Ramsay opened a number of highly acclaimed restaurants across the world before becoming a television star in the late 1990s on the British show Ramsay's Kitchen Nightmares. He launched an American version, Hell's Kitchen, in 2007.
